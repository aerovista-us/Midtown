Simple Buyer-Facing Explanation

The agreement is set up so your initial payment of $17,376.60 covers the immediate reinstatement/cure amount and serves as nonrefundable consideration for the overall deal. From there, you would have the option to purchase the property at the agreed price, with the option staying available as long as the required payments and property-use obligations stay in good standing.

The monthly payment is set to match the current mortgage payment amount, so the property stays current during the option period. Seller keeps legal title until a valid option exercise and closing are completed. AeroVista would be the tenant/occupant side of the arrangement, but that does not give you ownership or possession before closing — your right is the purchase option, subject to the agreement terms.

The goal is to keep the property protected, keep payments current, avoid confusion about ownership before closing, and give you a clear path to purchase if everything performs as agreed.
